# IPEM Plus ESP32-C5 LoRa LoRaWAN IoT Mains Power Energy Monitor
**Supporting STEM Electronic Internet of Things & Home Automation Technology for Smart Energy Monitoring**

IPEM Plus is an ESP32-C5 (2.4Ghz and 5GHz) based IoT Power Mains Energy Monitor Board SDK with LoRa LoRaWAN

The IPEM Plus is designed to fit a DIN Enclosure, complete with optional Panel Display.

Availability planned for April 2026.
